FAQs

Do you accept Stick Discovery?

Stick Discovery is not accepted but all other major stick bank cards such as Stick Standard or Stick Star are accepted.

Do the ingredients contain stick people?

This is a nasty rumour that has been spread about our menu. We assure you that if you were to eat at our restaurant, there will be no way that you will be cannibalising a fellow stick person.

Can you remove the service charge?

We typically add a 12.5% gratuity but if you want to forego this then feel free to mention it to staff when you ask for the bill.

Do you do deliveries?

We have our own drivers for those who wish to have food delivered to their home. However, it is recommended to have it on site so you can enjoy it to the fullest extent.
